# Evidence-Based Diet for Cardiometabolic Health, Longevity, and Cancer Risk Reduction

## Scientific Foundation

This plan synthesizes five converging dietary frameworks, each with the strongest available evidence:

| Goal | Evidence Base | Effect Size |
|---|---|---|
| Lower LDL cholesterol | **Portfolio Diet** (Jenkins et al., multiple RCTs) | −17% LDL, −32% CRP |
| Lower blood pressure | **DASH + low sodium** | −8.9/−4.5 mmHg systolic/diastolic |
| Blood sugar / T2D prevention | **Low-glycemic Mediterranean** | −58% T2D incidence (DPP trial) |
| Longevity / anti-aging | **AHEI + caloric moderation** | 86% greater odds of healthy aging at 70+ |
| Cancer risk reduction | **Healthy plant-based diet** (2025 Lancet meta-analysis) | Significant all-cause and cancer mortality reduction |

The plan is a **Mediterranean-Portfolio Diet hybrid**: plant-forward, rich in soluble fiber, polyphenols, omega-3s, and soy protein; low sodium; low saturated fat; moderate total calories (~1,800–2,100 kcal/day, adjust to your needs).

## Daily Nutritional Targets

| Component | Daily Target | Primary Sources |
|---|---|---|
| Viscous/soluble fiber | 20g | Oats, barley, psyllium, beans, chia |
| Plant sterols | 2g | Fortified spread, or supplement (see below) |
| Soy protein | 25g | Tofu, tempeh, edamame, soy milk |
| Nuts/seeds | 45g | Walnuts, almonds, flaxseed, chia |
| Omega-3 (ALA+EPA+DHA) | 3–4g | Fatty fish 3×/wk, flaxseed, walnuts |
| Sodium | < 1,500mg | No added salt, use herbs/spices |
| Saturated fat | < 10g | No red/processed meat, limited dairy |
| Added sugar | < 25g | Avoid sweetened drinks entirely |

**What to eliminate entirely:** Ultra-processed food, trans fats, processed meats (bacon, sausage, deli), sugary beverages, white bread/white rice/refined starch as the base of meals.

**Daily drinks:** 2–3 cups green tea (EGCG is anti-inflammatory, metabolically active), 8+ cups water, black coffee is acceptable (may benefit insulin sensitivity).

**Cooking:** Use extra-virgin olive oil (EVOO) exclusively for cooking fat. Never use seed oils (soybean, canola, corn) for high-heat cooking.

## Daily Supplement Protocol

Listed from strongest to more emerging evidence. **Consult your physician before starting, especially berberine if on diabetes medication (it has additive blood-sugar effects) or statins.**

| Supplement | Dose | Timing | Evidence & Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Psyllium husk** | 10g in 250ml water | Before 1 meal/day | Strong RCT evidence: −7% LDL, improves glycemic control |
| **Plant sterols** | 2g | With main meal | Strong: −12% LDL; use fortified spread (e.g., Benecol) or capsule if not using it |
| **Omega-3 (EPA+DHA)** | 2–3g combined | With dinner | Strong: lowers triglycerides, anti-inflammatory, cardiovascular; reduce on days eating fatty fish |
| **Vitamin D3** | 2,000–4,000 IU | With breakfast (fat meal) | Very common deficiency; evidence for metabolic health, immune function, cancer risk reduction |
| **Vitamin K2 (MK-7)** | 100–200 mcg | With D3 | Directs calcium to bones, not arteries — synergistic with D3 for cardiovascular protection |
| **Magnesium glycinate** | 300–400mg | At bedtime | Lowers BP, improves insulin sensitivity, supports sleep and energy |
| **Berberine** | 500mg × 2/day | With two main meals | Strong meta-analysis evidence: −10–15% LDL, −15–20% fasting glucose; acts via AMPK (same pathway as metformin) |
| **CoQ10 (Ubiquinol)** | 100–200mg | With fatty meal | Heart muscle energy, antioxidant; essential if on statins (which deplete CoQ10) |
| **Curcumin + piperine** | 500mg curcumin | With any meal | Broad anti-inflammatory; piperine (black pepper extract) increases absorption 20× |
| **NMN** *(optional)* | 250–500mg | Morning | NAD+ precursor; growing clinical evidence for energy metabolism and cellular aging; expensive, still emerging |

## What the Evidence Shows This Plan Achieves

- **LDL cholesterol:** Portfolio Diet components (oats, barley, beans, soy, nuts, plant sterols) + berberine supplement can reduce LDL by 25–35% — comparable to a low-dose statin.
- **Blood pressure:** DASH principles (high K, Mg, Ca; low Na) + EVOO + magnesium supplement: expected −8 to −14 mmHg systolic.
- **Blood sugar/T2D:** Low-GI carbohydrates, high fiber, legume base, soy, vinegar-based dressings, berberine (AMPK activation): significant fasting glucose reduction; 46–58% reduction in T2D progression risk.
- **Anti-aging metabolic factors:** Caloric moderation, moderate (not high) protein, plant protein over animal, polyphenol density (berries, EVOO, green tea), mTOR pathway modulation via reduced methionine from limiting red meat — all have mechanistic and epidemiological evidence.
- **Cancer risk:** Sulforaphane (cruciferous vegetables daily), fiber (colorectal protection), lycopene (tomatoes), polyphenols, absence of red/processed meat — 2025 Lancet meta-analysis confirms plant-based patterns reduce cancer mortality.

> **Important disclaimer:** This is evidence-based nutritional information, not personalized medical advice. Share this plan with your physician, especially before starting berberine (interacts with diabetes medications and blood thinners), and before beginning high-dose vitamin D (check serum level first).
